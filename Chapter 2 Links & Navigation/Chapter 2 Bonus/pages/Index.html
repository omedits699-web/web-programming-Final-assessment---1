The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 American superhero film based on the DC Comics character Batman. It is the second installment in Christopher Nolan's Dark Knight trilogy and the 22nd film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Directed by Christopher Nolan and written by Jonathan Nolan and David S. Goyer, the film features an ensemble cast including Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart, Michael Caine, Maggie Gyllenhaal, Tom Hardy, and Gary Oldman.
