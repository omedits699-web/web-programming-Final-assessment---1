The Plot

The Dark Knight is about Batman fighting crime in Gotham City.

What happens

After the Joker causes chaos in Gotham, Batman must stop him and protect the city.

He faces many challenges and must make difficult decisions to save the city.

In the end, Batman succeeds in stopping the Joker, but at a great personal cost.

The End

It is a very sad ending but the city is safe.
