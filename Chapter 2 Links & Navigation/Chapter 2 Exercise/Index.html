The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 American superhero film based on DC Comics character Batman. It is the second installment in Christopher Nolan's Dark Knight trilogy and features an ensemble cast including Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart, Michael Caine, Maggie Gyllenhaal, and Gary Oldman.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release: 2008

Genre: Superhero/Action

Runtime: 152 minutes
