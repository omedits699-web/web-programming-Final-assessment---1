Characters

Main Characters

Meet the iconic characters who define the battle for Gotham's soul.

Batman (Hero)

Bruce Wayne's alter ego, Gotham's protector who walks the line between justice and vengeance. He's determined, strategic, and highly skilled in combat.

The Joker (Villain)

A chaotic criminal mastermind who wants to watch Gotham burn and test Batman's moral code. He's unpredictable, dangerous, and manipulative.

Harvey Dent (Anti-Hero)

Gotham's "White Knight" District Attorney who becomes the tragic Two-Face. He's ambitious but ultimately torn between good and evil.

Alfred Pennyworth (Ally)

Bruce Wayne's loyal butler, father figure, and moral compass in the fight for Gotham. He's wise, dedicated, and always supportive.

Rachel Dawes (Ally)

Bruce Wayne's childhood friend and Harvey Dent's fiancée, caught between two worlds. She's principled and intelligent.

James Gordon (Ally)

Policeman who works with Batman to clean up Gotham. He's honest and committed to justice.

Central Theme

The fine line between hero and villain, order and chaos.
