Reviews

Critical Reception

The Dark Knight received universal acclaim from critics and audiences alike. The film holds a 94% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes and is considered one of the greatest superhero films ever made.

Professional Reviews

Roger Ebert - Chicago Sun-Times

"The Dark Knight" is not a comic book movie. It's a crime epic. It's a tragedy. It's a meditation on heroism in a post-9/11 world. It's a film that will be studied and discussed for years to come.

Rating: ★★★★★

Peter Travers - Rolling Stone

Ledger's Joker is a landmark performance. It's terrifying, mesmerizing, and utterly unforgettable. This is the superhero movie that transcends the genre.

Rating: ★★★★★

A.O. Scott - The New York Times

Christopher Nolan has made a movie that is both a thrilling spectacle and a serious work of art. The Dark Knight raises the bar for what a comic book movie can be.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Audience Reception

The film was a massive commercial success, grossing over $1 billion worldwide. Audiences praised Heath Ledger's performance, the film's dark tone, and its complex themes of heroism and chaos.

Awards and Accolades

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor (Heath Ledger)
  • Academy Award for Best Sound Editing
  • Nominated for 8 total Academy Awards
  • BAFTA Award for Best Supporting Actor (Heath Ledger)
  • Golden Globe for Best Supporting Actor (Heath Ledger)
  • AFI's Top 10 Films of 2008

Legacy

The Dark Knight is widely regarded as one of the most influential films of the 2000s. It redefined what superhero films could achieve and set a new standard for the genre. The film's exploration of themes like chaos, morality, and heroism continues to be studied and admired by filmmakers and audiences alike.
