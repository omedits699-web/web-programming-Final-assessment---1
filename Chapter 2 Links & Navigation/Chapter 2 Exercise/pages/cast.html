Cast & Creative Team

Main Cast

  • Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
  • Heath Ledger as The Joker
  • Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face
  • Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth
  • Gary Oldman as Lieutenant Jim Gordon
  • Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes
  • Morgan Freeman as Lucius Fox
  • Eric Roberts as Salvatore Maroni

Creative Team

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Writer: Jonathan Nolan, David S. Goyer
  • Producer: Christopher Nolan, Emma Thomas, Charles Roven
  • Cinematography: Wally Pfister
  • Music: Hans Zimmer, James Newton Howard
  • Editing: Lee Smith
  • Production Design: Nathan Crowley
  • Costume Design: Lindy Hemming

Notable Performances

Heath Ledger's performance as the Joker is widely regarded as one of the greatest villain performances in cinema history. Ledger posthumously won the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role, becoming the first actor to win an Oscar for a superhero film.

Christian Bale's portrayal of Batman was praised for its depth and complexity, while Aaron Eckhart's transformation into Two-Face provided a compelling tragic element to the story.
