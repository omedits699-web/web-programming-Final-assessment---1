Cast

Main Cast Members

An amazing cast bringing to life the complex characters of Gotham's darkest chapter.

Christian Bale as Batman

The Dark Knight himself, torn between heroism and vengeance.

Heath Ledger as The Joker

An iconic, Oscar-winning performance as the agent of chaos.

Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent

Gotham's "White Knight" who becomes Two-Face.

Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth

Bruce Wayne's loyal butler and moral compass.

Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes

Bruce Wayne's childhood friend and Harvey Dent's love interest.

Gary Oldman as James Gordon

Policeman who works with Batman to clean up Gotham.

Achievement

Academy Award Winner: Heath Ledger (Best Supporting Actor, posthumous)
