Green Tea Recipe

Perfect Brew for Beach Days

Green tea is a healthy beverage made from tea leaves. It comes from China and has many health benefits. Green tea is good for your body and helps you feel calm and refreshed, especially on a beautiful beach day.

This tea has antioxidants that support your health. You can drink it hot or cold. It tastes great and helps you relax while enjoying the ocean breeze.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh water
  • 1 green tea bag
  • Honey or lemon (optional)
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Boil water to 80-85°C (175-185°F)
  2. Place tea bag in your favorite cup
  3. Pour hot water gently over tea bag
  4. Steep for exactly 2-3 minutes for perfect flavor
  5. Remove tea bag gently
  6. Add honey or lemon to taste if desired
  7. Stir well and enjoy your beach tea!
